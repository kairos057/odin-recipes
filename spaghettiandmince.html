Spaghetti and Mince

Description

This is very meaty and not near as acid as most sauces. It's really worth the time. It makes a very large batch, so I often freeze half.

    Ingredients

    • 1 packet (500g) Spaghetti
    • 500g Beef mince
    • 1 large Onion, finely chopped
    • 1 tablespoon Crushed garlic
    • 1 can (400g) Chopped tomatoes
    • 2 tablespoons Tomato paste
    • 2 tablespoons Mrs. Ball’s Chutney (for that local sweetness)
    • 3 tablespoons All Gold Tomato Sauce
    • 1 Knorr Beef Stock Pot (or Royco Savoury Mince packet)
    • 1 teaspoon Robertsons Dried Mixed Herbs
    • Grated Cheddar cheese (for serving)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to the boil, add the spaghetti, and cook until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat a splash of oil in a large pan over medium heat. Fry the chopped onion and crushed garlic until soft and translucent.
  3. Add the beef mince to the pan, breaking it up with a wooden spoon, and fry until it is completely browned.
  4. Stir in the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, chutney, All Gold tomato sauce, beef stock, and mixed herbs. Simmer on low heat for 15-20 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  5. Serve the rich mince sauce spooned over the warm spaghetti, and top generously with grated Cheddar cheese.
